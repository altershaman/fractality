# Consensus

When two or more persons independently hold the same judgment, something shifts — not in the structure of either BB, but in the inter-personal status of the belief. It moves from private belief to **shared knowledge**.

This elevation is the global purpose of communication. Consensus does not require agreement on everything — only on specific judgments, one at a time. It is built gradually, through communicative acts, negotiation, and mutual acquisition.

While inter-personal sharing knowledge is not stored anywhere separately and exists in the virtual overlap between BBs — distributed in decentralized manner, Consensus is public manifest of agree on some number of judgments. Statement of common truth and invitation to join.
